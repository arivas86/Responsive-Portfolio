About Me

A grumpy U.S. Navy Veteran who sometimes has the ability to show a grin. I spend most of my day working, but when I get a chance I like to play videogames, go to the gym, and eat pizza.

Music Festivals are my favorite thing to do in the world. I have traveled to the other side of the world in search of the ultimae music experience. There is nothing that compares to the energy and euphoria you get from the music blasting from the speakers sorrounded by thousands of people united as one singing and dancing their worries away.
